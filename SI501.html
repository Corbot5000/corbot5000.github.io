InsideOut Literary Arts

Contextual Inquiry and Consulting

Throughout the semester, my team and I consulted with a local creative arts non-profit called the InsideOut Literary Arts Project. InsideOut consists of a team of 10 employees who are constantly collaborating on projects like grant proposals and slam poetry events.

Once we used the contextual inquiry process to understand InsideOut’s process and workflow, we got to work and created an affinity wall that helped us understand the issues employees face. Many of these concerns involved backing up and archiving important documents, along with collaborating in more productive ways.

Finally, we delivered a detailed analysis of how InsideOut can improve its operations by utilizing Microsoft Office 365 and Microsoft SharePoint, including tips on how the system could be implemented with InsideOut’s needs in mind.
